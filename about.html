How the Dashboard Works

The dashboard runs entirely in your browser. When you upload a CSV file, it is processed on your device to generate charts and tables. No data is sent to a server.

Your Data Stays Private

We do not collect, store, or transmit your data. Everything happens locally, and closing the page removes all uploaded information.

Why it's Safe to Use

  • Open-source code that you can inspect.
  • Runs offline once loaded; no hidden network requests.
  • Data is kept in memory only for visualization and never saved.
